An Average Day of College

Chapter 2

You arrive to college at roughly 9.30am, nerves begin to rise as you stare down the double door to the lecture hall. After wiping the sweat from your brow you muster the courage and swing open the door, all eyes fall upon you and to make matters worse as you enter the room you notice just to your right the lecturer is staring right at you with a cold look almost questioning why you'd bother coming late.
